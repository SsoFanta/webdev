Indianapolis

Weather

Indiana has very unpredictable weather, and it often would go from sunny to rainy suddenly, or from cloudy to bright and shining.

Location

Indianapolis is in the center of Indiana, surrounded by states like Ohio, Illinois, Michigan, and others.

Fun Facts

  • Indianapolis has more than 800,000 residents
  • It is the home of the Indy 500
  • The largest childrens museum is in Indiapolis
