Attractions of Indianapolis

Childrens Museum of Indianapolis

The Children's Museum in Indianapolis is the largest children's museum in the entire world, and features over 15 exhibits with historical artifacts.

Indianapolis Motor Speedway

The speedway is home to one of the largest racing events in the USA, the Indy 500, with an average of 7 million viewers.

Indianapolis Zoo

The zoo in Indianapolis is a favorite of locals, always being packed with visitors and being home to over 300 species of animals.

Lucas Oil Stadium

Home of the Indianapolis Colts, has hosted the nfl scouting combine for 19+ years.
